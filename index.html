    Self-care

    Self-care means taking the time to do things that help you live well and improve both your physical health and mental health.

    Why is it important?

    This can help you manage stress, lower your risk of illness, and increase your energy. Even small acts of self-care in your daily life can have a big impact.


    Join Support Groups!

    One powerful yet often overlooked way to take care of yourself is by joining support groups. These communities allow you to connect with people who share similar backgrounds and aspects of identity, fostering a sense of understanding and belonging. In addition to emotional support, many of these groups offer valuable resources tailored to your specific needs.
