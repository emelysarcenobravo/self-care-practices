## What's in this project?

← `README.md`: All about the website! 

← `index.html`: Introduces the concept of self-care, explaining what it is, why it matters, and how it impacts overall health.  

← `habits.html`: Outlines effective self-care strategies such as meditation, physical activity, and journaling, which are designed to help users build healthier routines.

← `support-groups.html`: Offers detailed information about support groups in San Francisco, including their mission, location, and the services they provide and communities they serve, helping users find the right fit for their needs.

← `style.css`: Adds styling to my content.

← `script.js`: Adds interactivity to my web app.
